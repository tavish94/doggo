Scout

Many families find that the work of taking care of a dog is unequally distributed within the family. Parents want a way to get their children more invested in the caretaking process while also increasing accountability for these responsibilities. Their children want incentives to help care for the dog as well as reminders about what the dog needs. Everyone in the family wants to ensure that their dog receives the best care possible.
